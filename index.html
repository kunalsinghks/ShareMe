How ShareME Works

Direct Bridge

ShareME creates an encrypted tunnel directly from your PC to a public URL. No cloud servers store your data.

Infinite Limits

Transfer 10GB, 50GB, or even 1TB files. Your only limit is your local drive space and upload speed.

One-Scan Share

Built-in QR engine generates codes instantly. Scan from any mobile device to start the high-speed transfer.

100% Open Source

Full Transparency

Audit the code yourself. Every line is open to the community on GitHub under the MIT License.

Built for Devs

Coded in Python with FastAPI and CustomTkinter. Easy to fork and customise for your own needs.

Global Security

Leveraging Cloudflare's world-class E2EE tunnel infrastructure for data in transit.
