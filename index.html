How it Works

01

Select Files

Drag and drop files or folders into the ShareME interface. They remain on your device.

02

Start Sharing

Click "Start Sharing". ShareME initializes a secure, temporary public URL (e.g., https://random-name.trycloudflare.com).

03

Share Link

Send the link or show the QR code to anyone. They can view and download files directly from their browser.
